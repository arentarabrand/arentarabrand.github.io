About Arentara

Arentara is more than just palm sugar. It’s a commitment to nature, culture, and purity. Sourced from the lush palm forests of South Cianjur, every product tells a story of heritage and sustainability.

Our Mission

To bring the world closer to nature by providing premium, organic palm sugar while empowering local communities.

Our Vision

To be the global icon of authentic Indonesian palm products through innovation, integrity, and sustainability.
